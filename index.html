How it works
Order acknowledgement: Upload the PDF from Marmon — creates a new record with SO#, customer PO#, scheduled ship date, line items, and total.
Invoice: Upload the invoice PDF — matches to the existing order by Sales Order #, then updates qty shipped, balance, actual ship date, and invoice number. Multiple invoices (partial shipments) are all tracked.
OneDrive sync: All data saves to your OneDrive automatically so it's available on every computer you sign into.
